About ResQMeal

ResQMeal is an innovative solution to reduce food waste and save money by providing recipes based on the ingredients you already have at home. Our goal is to help students and ecologically conscious individuals make the most of their food while minimizing waste.

World and National Problematics

In France, more than 10 million tons of food are wasted each year, resulting in 16 billion euros of waste. Students often struggle to afford food, leading to increased food waste and reliance on charities, which have seen a 150% rise in demand.

Globally, food waste contributes to a 3.3 billion ton carbon footprint, highlighting the need for collective action to reduce waste and promote sustainability.

Our Project

ResQMeal aims to create an app that suggests recipes based on the ingredients you have at home, helping you save money and reduce waste. By using our app, users can make the most of their groceries, avoid unnecessary purchases, and contribute to a greener planet.

Our Early Adopters

Our initial target audience includes scholarship students from EFREI who want to save money and reduce food waste, as well as ecologically conscious individuals and organizations seeking sustainable solutions.

Observations

We observed an EFREI student with financial difficulties struggling to optimize his cooking and grocery shopping, generating significant waste at home. This highlighted the need for a solution like ResQMeal.
